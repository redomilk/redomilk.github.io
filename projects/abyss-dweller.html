Overview

Project summary

Abyss Dweller is a solo project where I owned concept, direction, and implementation. The goal was to create a compelling gameplay loop supported by strong atmosphere and clear player feedback.

Role

Responsibilities

  • Concept and systems design
  • Core gameplay implementation
  • Iteration and scope refinement
  • Presentation and polish

Process

Design approach

As a solo project, the process involved balancing scope with ambition while keeping the experience readable and focused. I prioritized building a playable core loop early, then improving feel and clarity through iteration.

Takeaways

What I learned

This project pushed me to make stronger design decisions independently, especially around scope, prioritization, and how to support player understanding through feedback.
