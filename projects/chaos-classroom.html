Spring 2024 – Spring 2025 Team project Encounter design Iteration

Overview

Project summary

Chaos Classroom was a team-based project focused on building a cohesive gameplay experience through repeated testing and iteration. My work centered on shaping encounters, improving readability, and supporting the overall player experience.

Role

My contributions

  • Designed and tuned encounters
  • Iterated on gameplay pacing through testing
  • Collaborated across design and development
  • Helped refine clarity and game feel

Process

How it evolved

Early versions of the game revealed friction around readability, challenge spikes, and player understanding. Through playtests, we adjusted pacing, feedback, and layout decisions to make the experience clearer and more engaging.

Takeaways

What I learned

This project reinforced the value of team communication, rapid iteration, and using playtest feedback to improve systems instead of only polishing presentation.
