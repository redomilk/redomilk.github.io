What I focus on

I care about how mechanics teach themselves, how encounters create decision pressure, and how systems communicate clearly to players.

  • Systems design and tuning
  • Rapid prototyping
  • Playtest-driven iteration
  • Readable UX and game feel

How I work

I like taking a concept from rough prototype to polished loop, using testing to find what is actually fun, understandable, and worth keeping.

  • Build a playable slice early
  • Observe friction points
  • Adjust the system, not just the surface
  • Document why changes matter
